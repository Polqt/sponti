Account and Data Deletion Instructions

App: Sponti

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Sponti and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Choose Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access your account, email poyhidalgo@gmail.com with subject line: Sponti Account Deletion Request.

Include the email address used in your Sponti account.

What gets deleted

We will delete your account and associated profile data, subject to legal, security, and fraud-prevention requirements.
